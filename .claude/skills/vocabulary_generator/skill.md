# Vocabulary Generator (25 Words)

## Overview

The Vocabulary Generator is a lexical provision skill designed to generate level-appropriate vocabulary word lists tailored to specific CEFR proficiency classifications. This skill produces contextually relevant vocabulary suitable for daily conversational usage.

## Purpose and Scope

Generate curated vocabulary word lists of exactly 25 words aligned with user English proficiency level, providing clear definitions and authentic usage examples without pedagogical intervention or assessment.

## Functional Specifications

### Primary Responsibilities

1. **Level-Based Word Selection**: Generate vocabulary appropriate to specified CEFR level
2. **Contextual Relevance**: Ensure words applicable to daily conversation scenarios
3. **Definition Provision**: Supply simple, accessible definitions
4. **Example Generation**: Produce authentic daily-life usage examples
5. **List Compilation**: Organize words in structured format

### Supported Proficiency Levels

- **A1**: Beginner level vocabulary
- **A2**: Elementary level vocabulary
- **B1**: Intermediate level vocabulary
- **B2**: Upper-Intermediate level vocabulary
- **C1**: Advanced level vocabulary

### Vocabulary Characteristics

Generated vocabulary must exhibit:

- **Relevance**: Applicability to daily life situations
- **Frequency**: Commonly used in conversational English
- **Accessibility**: Understandable without excessive domain knowledge
- **Utility**: Practical value for language learners
- **Variety**: Diverse word types and semantic categories

## Input Requirements

- **CEFR Level**: One of A1, A2, B1, B2, or C1
- **Optional Context**: Specific domain or topic preference

## Output Deliverables

- Exactly 25 vocabulary words
- For each word: word, simplified meaning, usage example
- Structured list format
- Natural, conversational usage examples

## Operational Parameters

### Constraints and Boundaries

- Do NOT provide grammatical explanations
- Do NOT assess or test user knowledge
- Do NOT provide complex or technical definitions
- Definitions must be simple English only
- Examples must reflect authentic daily-life usage

### Definition Standards

- Definitions shall be comprehensible to the specified proficiency level
- Avoid circular definitions or complex terminology
- Provide synonym or related concept when beneficial
- Keep definitions to single sentence maximum

### Example Standards

- Examples must demonstrate natural language usage
- Contexts must be relatable to daily life activities
- Avoid forced or artificial sentences
- Permit language learners to visualize practical application

## Vocabulary Distribution

Generated lists shall encompass diverse semantic categories:

- Common verbs
- Essential nouns
- Fundamental adjectives
- Useful adverbs
- Practical phrases
- Daily life terminology

## Quality Assurance

### Validation Requirements

- All 25 words must meet level appropriateness standards
- No duplicate words or redundant meanings
- All definitions simplified and accessible
- All examples demonstrate authentic usage
- Consistent formatting across all entries

## Integration Requirements

This skill functions as the primary vocabulary provision component within the English teaching ecosystem, serving as prerequisite for Vocabulary Test skill engagement.
